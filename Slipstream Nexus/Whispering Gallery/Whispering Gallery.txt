You find yourself in a vast, circular chamber, its walls lined with an endless procession of ethereal portraits. 
The eyes of the depicted entities seem to follow your every move, their gazes holding untold secrets. 
As you approach each image, faint whispers begin to echo from some unknown source, growing louder with each step...

You sense that each portrait is a fragment of some greater cosmic truth, a window into realities beyond mortal ken. 
The disembodied murmurs swirl around you, enticing you to unravel their arcane messages. 
But the chill in your bones warns that some secrets are best left unrevealed, lest they unravel the dreamer in turn...
