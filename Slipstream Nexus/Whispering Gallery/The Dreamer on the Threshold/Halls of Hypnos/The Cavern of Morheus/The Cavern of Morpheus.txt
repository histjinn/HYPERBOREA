You step through the shimmering portal and find yourself in a vast, dimly lit cavern. 
The air is thick with the scent of poppies and the distant sound of gently lapping water. 
Shadows dance along the rocky walls, seeming to take on a life of their own as they shift and merge in the flickering light of bioluminescent fungi.

At the center of the cavern lies an immense, obsidian pool - the legendary dream-spring of Morpheus himself. 
Its surface is perfectly still, reflecting the cavern's otherworldly glow like a liquid mirror. 
You feel an irresistible pull towards its inky depths, as if the pool is a gateway to the very heart of the dreaming...
