You step through the shimmering portal and find yourself in a tranquil, dreamlike space. 
The air itself seems to vibrate with a soothing, pulsing energy. 
Soft, ambient light emanates from no discernible source, suffusing everything in a gentle, hypnagogic glow. 
Before you lie three pools of swirling, iridescent liquid, each promising a different flavor of oneiric revelation.

This is the inner sanctum of Somnus, the gentle bringer of restful repose. 
Here, the dreaming mind may sip from the fount of self-knowledge, confront long-buried yearnings, or catch ephemeral glimpses of what is yet to come. 
Tread lightly, for in the realm of sleep, even the most innocent vision may prove a doorway to awakening...
